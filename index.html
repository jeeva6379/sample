14 October

In the extensive history of their cricket rivalry spanning countless matches across various formats, India has often emerged victorious in crucial ICC tournaments and World Cup encounters, showcasing their prowess on the global stage. Conversely, Pakistan has maintained an edge in the overall number of matches won, reflecting their resilience and consistency over the years. The anticipation escalates as these cricketing powerhouses, India and Pakistan, are set to clash on the grand stage of the 2023 ICC Cricket World Cup at the illustrious Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad on the momentous date of October 14, 2023.
